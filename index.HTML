Donald J. Trump

45th President of the United States

Donald J. Trump served as the 45th President of the United States from 2017 to 2021. Known for his larger-than-life personality, he revolutionized American politics.

Businessman & Entrepreneur

Before his presidency, Trump built a global business empire with interests in real estate, entertainment, and media.

Trump's Influence

Love him or hate him, Donald Trump left a lasting impact on the political landscape of the U.S. and the world.
